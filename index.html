Accident Reconstruction & Injury Biomechanics

Attorneys Ask

When litigation involves accidents and injuries, attorneys have technical questions.

  • Attorneys often ask:
  • Q: Was the vehicle speeding?
  • Q: Was the driver wearing a seat belt?
  • Q: How did the occupant move?
  • Q: What do the witness marks indicate?
  • Q: When was the occupant ejected?
  • Q: When did the injury occur?
  • Q: What was the mechanism of injury?

Attorneys want to understand how the environment, vehicle, and human body interconnect in an accident. Moreover, attorneys need to know if the accident forces were sufficient to cause the injury.

To know which forces cause injury and which forces do not, attorneys need biomechanical engineering insight.
